Factions / Kaizoku, 海賊

PIRATES

Pirates, or Kaizoku, are adventurers of the seas who defy the World Government in pursuit of freedom, treasure, and dreams. While some are notorious marauders, others, like the Straw Hats, sail for ideals and adventure. Each crew’s Jolly Roger reflects its captain’s spirit, and their names echo through wanted posters that inspire both fear and admiration across the world.

Factions / Kurosu Girudo, クロスギルド

CROSS-GUILD

Cross Guild, led in name by Buggy the Genius Jester but truly powered by Mihawk and Crocodile, emerged from the ashes of the abolished Seven Warlords of the Sea—a force once ranked just below the Four Emperors. By placing bounties on Marines instead of pirates, they turned justice upside down, spreading fear throughout the Navy ranks. With former Warlords at its core, Cross Guild has quickly become one of the most dangerous wild cards in the current era.

Factions / Kakumeigun, 革命軍

REVOLUTIONARY ARMY

The Revolutionary Army, founded and led by Monkey D. Dragon, is the only global organization openly opposing the World Government’s centuries-old rule. Unlike pirates who seek personal freedom, they aim to overthrow tyranny and free oppressed nations. With leaders like Sabo the Flame Emperor and a network of secret divisions, they embody rebellion, hope, and the spark of worldwide change.

Factions / Kaigun, 海軍

MARINES

The Marines, or Kaigun, are the World Government’s military enforcers, led today by Fleet Admiral Akainu and the new Admirals Fujitora, Ryokugyu, and Kizaru. Admirals are considered the Navy’s ultimate military force, capable of changing the outcome of wars with their sheer presence. Yet while they claim to uphold “justice,” divisions in ideals and corruption within the system often blur their role as protectors, leaving the Marines as both order’s shield and a tool of oppression.

Factions / Gorosei to Imu-sama, 五老星とイム様

IMU & THE FIVE ELDERS

At the peak of power sits Imu, the hidden “Highest Authority” whose existence is unknown to the public, while the Gorosei act as the visible rulers. They control the Marines, the Cipher Pol, and even the flow of history by erasing truths from the world. Though presented as wise leaders, their true power is shrouded in secrecy, tied to the mysteries of the Void Century and the ancient weapons.
